About

A personal, technical, and purpose-driven story.

I study Data Science and Bioengineering at UC Berkeley, where my work lives at the intersection of computation, biology, and human impact. I’m especially interested in building pipelines that don’t just produce answers, but help people understand how those answers were reached.
